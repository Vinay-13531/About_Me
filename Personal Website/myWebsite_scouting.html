SCOUTING


I have been pursuing scouting starting from the beginning of Cub scouts. I started from 2nd grade all the way uptil now. In cub scouts, I have achieved the ranks, Bobcat, Tiger, Wolf, Bear, and Webelos. In Boy scouts I have acheived the ranks Scout, Tenderfoot and Second Class so far. I am only one requirement away from first class. I have earned a total of 41 merit badges so far during Boyscouts.


In scouting, we all do many different events together as a troop. In my patrol, The Used Cars, there are eight of us. I am currently the patrol leader of our patrol. Some of the events we do as a troop are fishing, camping, backpacking, hiking, rock climbing and much more. I have enjoyed Boy Scouts and it is a really big part of my life.
